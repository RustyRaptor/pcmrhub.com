Welcome Fellow PC Enthusiasts!

Beginning as a humble facebook group PC Master Race aims to bring PC gamers together.

Everyone is here to have a good time.

The reason for this group is to bring good people with similar interests together. While the PC Master Race name is sattire this group is by no means limited to only PC Gamers/Enthusiasts. Anyone with any interest or questions regarding this group should not hesitate to speak with our staff! Without a community we are nothing and we plan to keep it that way.

What kind of hardware/software do you need?

This seems to be a commonly asked question among beginner/veteran pc users. Honestly there is no all encompassing answer to this, depending on each person's individual needs will determine their "requirements". This is where our group comes in! Within these pages are hours upon hours spent testing,benchmarking, and generally using all types of equipment. What items you want to use are going to depend on many different variables and this enthusiast ran community is the perfect place to find that solution.

PCMRHub Community

There is a neverending hunger to grow our community because.... ya know, the more people we have the more opinions,interests,help can be spread. One thing we are excited about is our streaming potential and including our community with us. Once a week there will be a staff streaming day that will include members of PCMRHub to join them in their endeavors. Regarding streaming there is allocated time for each day where our members can stream and briefly advertise their twitch/youtube/facebook pages. Your opinion's matter, if there are any suggestions or recommendations let us know! -PCMRHub Staff
