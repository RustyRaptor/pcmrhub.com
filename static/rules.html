The following behaviors are prohibited on the Facebook group.

  • The use of a language that isn’t English.

  • Hate and discrimination against console players.

  • Bullying, harassment, and discrimination against individuals or a group of individuals.\

  • Making posts that are off-topic and completely unrelated to PCs, Gaming, and technology.

  • Hating and bashing anything without a written explanation as to why you think it's bad (for example, posting a meme showing someone roasting a chicken over an amd processor)

  • Advertising your content or services without making a donation to the group. (details on the ‘donate’ page)

  • Posting or commenting pornographic material or anything that conflicts with the Facebook Terms of service. (you can read them here )

  • Infringing upon another member’s privacy by exposing their personal information.

  • Spamming and flooding posts and comments with unnecessary content.

  • Creating open polls. All polls must be closed such that other members can’t add options.

  • Posting a question that can be easily google searched. If you can’t find an answer on google please specify that otherwise it will be deleted and you may be banned.

  • Creating posts with the intention of upsetting/baiting a group of members.

  • Saying things that are racist, homophobic, sexist, or discriminatory to any minority or ethnic group in any way.

  • Hating on Linux.
